For the Iosonouncane & Daniela Pes European Tour, we developed a bespoke live visual system that responded in real time to both curated imagery and the evolving energy of the performance. Using TouchDesigner as the core engine for the VFX and Resoulme Arena as Video Mixer, we created dynamic visual effects that were driven in real-time, allowing us to manipulate and morph selected images during the show.

Our approach to the camera processing wanted to work with the vision of the artists and the direction, creating a softness of the shapes, that could blend one into the other.

These visuals were mapped across a custom LED wall configuration consisting of one expansive horizontal display complemented by two IMAGS, creating a panoramic visual field that wrapped the audience in a cohesive visual environment.
